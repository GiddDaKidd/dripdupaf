About

Revive Communities , Reestablish Businesses, and Renew Culture.

Who We Are

The Hands Of Honey foundation is a non for profit social venture capitalist firm. We act as both a financial intermediary and marketing platform for aspiring or established entrepreneurs interested in ensconcing their business within their communities.

Our Mission

We work to engender a knowledge based economy as an effort to transform disadvantaged communities into capital markets, building them up both culturally and economically.

How Entrepreneurs get involved

Entrepreneurs work with Hands of Honey to connect with investors looking to provide financial assistance and obtain necessary marketing tools to promote their business. We aim to connect with the most efficient, driven, and hard working entrepreneurs and business owners. We pride ourselves on working with the best of the best and we continue to search for others looking to make an impact in their community.
